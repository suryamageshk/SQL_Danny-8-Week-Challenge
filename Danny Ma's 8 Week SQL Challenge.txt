Case Study #1 - THE TASTE OF SUCCESS
-------------------------------------

Input tables:
* menu (product_id(PK), product_name, price) 
* members (customer_id(PK), join_date)
* sales (customer_id(FK), order_date, product_id(FK))

Table 1: Menu
The menu table has unique values of product_id and their respective product_name and price.

Table 2: Members
The members table has each and every customers with customer_id and join_date who joins Danny's dinner loyalty program. 

Table 3: Sales
The sales table captures all customers purchases with corresponding order_date and product_id.
